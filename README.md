# ☕ Coffee Sales Dashboard | Power BI
![Dashboard Preview](coffee-dashboard.png)
## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Coffee Sales Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard analyzes coffee sales performance across different products, years, months, and time periods to help identify sales trends and customer preferences.
The dashboard provides business insights into total sales, total orders, best-selling coffee types, monthly sales trends, and sales distribution across different times of the day.
---
## 🎯 Objective
The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze coffee sales performance
- Compare yearly sales
- Identify top-selling coffee products
- Monitor monthly sales trends
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Create an interactive dashboard for business decision-making
---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
### KPI Cards
- Total Orders
- Total Sales
- Peak Hour
- Supply Quantity
---
### Interactive Filters
Users can filter the dashboard by coffee type:
- Americano
- Americano with Milk
- Cappuccino
- Cocoa
- Cortado
- Espresso
- Hot Chocolate
- Latte
---
### Visualizations
#### 📈 Product Sales Comparison
Compares Supply vs Sales Amount for each coffee product.
---
#### 🏆 Best Selling Coffee Products
Ranks coffee products based on total orders.
---
#### 📅 Sales by Month
Displays monthly sales trends to identify seasonal demand.
---
#### 📊 Year-wise Sales Distribution
Compares sales performance between 2024 and 2025.
---
#### 🌞 Sales by Time of Day
Shows sales contribution during:
- Morning
- Afternoon
- Night
---
## 📈 Key Insights
- Total Sales reached **112.25K**.
- Around **4K** customer orders were placed.
- **Americano with Milk** is the highest-selling coffee.
- **Espresso** has the lowest sales.
- Most sales occurred during the **Morning**.
- Sales were highest in **March**.
- The majority of sales came from **2025** compared to 2024.
